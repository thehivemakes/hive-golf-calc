Converting Handicap Index to Course Handicap

Your handicap index is portable — it represents your potential ability. The course handicap adjusts for the difficulty of the specific course you are playing.

A harder course (higher slope) gives you more strokes. The formula: Course Handicap = Index × (Slope / 113) + (Course Rating − Par). For stroke play, use 95% of course handicap.
