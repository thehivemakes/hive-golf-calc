Swing Speed and Driving Distance

Club head speed is the primary determinant of driving distance. Every 1 mph of club speed adds approximately 2.5–3 yards of carry distance (with good contact).

Smash factor measures how efficiently energy transfers from the club to the ball. The theoretical maximum is 1.50. Most amateurs are between 1.40 and 1.48. Improving smash factor (hitting the center of the face) adds distance without swinging faster.
