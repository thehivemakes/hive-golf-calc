How Golf Handicap Index Works

The World Handicap System (WHS) calculates your handicap index from your best score differentials. A differential measures how you performed relative to the course difficulty.

With 20 rounds, the WHS uses the average of your best 8 differentials. With fewer rounds, it uses fewer differentials. This calculator provides an estimate — for an official handicap, register with your golf association.
