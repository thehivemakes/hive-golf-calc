How to Read Putting Break

Putting break depends on three factors: slope steepness, putt length, and green speed. Steeper slopes and faster greens produce more break. Longer putts break more because the ball is rolling slowly for a longer time.

This calculator provides a rough estimate. Real green reading also involves reading grain direction, identifying subtle undulations, and accounting for the ball's decelerating speed near the hole.
