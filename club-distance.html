Analyzing Your Club Distance Gaps

Distance gapping is the difference in carry distance between consecutive clubs. Consistent gaps of 10–15 yards give you full coverage of distances. Large gaps leave you "between clubs" too often.

If you have a 25-yard gap between your 3-wood and 5-iron, consider adding a hybrid or 4-iron. If two clubs go nearly the same distance, one may need adjusting or replacing.
